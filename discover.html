Our Story

Since 1946, Aspen Snowmass has been at the forefront of mountain recreation and responsible tourism in Colorado. What began as a single ski slope on Aspen Mountain has grown into a world-renowned resort with four unique mountains, exceptional accommodations, and a commitment to sustainability that sets the standard for the industry.

Our founder, Walter Paepcke, envisioned a place where visitors could nourish their minds, bodies, and spirits. Today, we continue that vision by offering not only incredible skiing and snowboarding but also cultural experiences, environmental stewardship, and community engagement.

Through the decades, we've evolved and expanded, but our core values remain the same: to provide unforgettable mountain experiences while preserving the natural beauty of the Roaring Fork Valley for future generations.

Our Mission

At Aspen Snowmass, we believe that a day on the mountain can change your life. Our mission is to provide exceptional experiences in spectacular settings, while leading the way in sustainable operations, environmental stewardship, and community development.

Sustainability Initiatives

Renewable Energy

Our resort operations are powered by 100% renewable electricity, and we've installed solar arrays on many of our facilities.

Forest Health

We actively manage our forests to improve wildlife habitat, reduce wildfire risk, and enhance forest resilience.

Water Conservation

We've implemented advanced snowmaking technology that uses less water and energy while producing better snow.

Community Programs

Our foundation supports environmental education and climate action throughout the Roaring Fork Valley.
